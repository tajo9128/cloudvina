## 1. Network Pharmacology (The "Systems Biology" Chapter)
*   **Why:** "Investigation of Alzheimer's Activity" isn't just about one target (AChE). Alzheimer's is complex.
*   **The Idea:** Map how your plant metabolities interact with the **entire gene network** of Alzheimer's (Inflammation, Apoptosis, Oxidative Stress).
*   **Methodology (Dry Lab):**
    *   **Databases:** STRING, GeneCards (Alzheimer's Genes), SwissTargetPrediction (Plant Targets).
    *   **Analysis:** Construct a **Compound-Target-Pathway Network** (using Cytoscape).
    *   **Outcome:** "We proved that *Evolvulus* acts not just on AChE, but also downregulates TNF-alpha (Inflammation) via Quercetin."
*   **Fit:** Perfectly enhances the **"Pharmacological Comparative Study"** aspect.

## 2. In Silico Clinical Trial Simulation (The "Futuristic" Chapter)
*   **Why:** You optimized a formulation (Pub 4). But will it work in humans?
*   **The Idea:** Run a "Virtual Clinical Trial" to predict variability in patient response.
*   **Methodology (Dry Lab):**
    *   **Tool:** Simcyp or OpenSystemsPharmacology (or BioDockify custom scripts).
    *   **Simulation:** Model a population of 100 virtual Alzheimer's patients (aged 65-85, varying liver function).
    *   **Input:** Your Lead Molecule's PK data.
    *   **Output:** "Predicted that 85% of patients will achieve therapeutic brain levels, but liver-impaired patients need dose adjustment."
*   **Fit:** Enhances **"Lead Formulation Optimization"** by adding direct translational value.

## 3. Green Synthesis of Nanoparticles (The "Green" Chapter)
*   **Why:** Integrating "Phytochemistry" with "Formulation" more tightly.
*   **The Idea:** Instead of just putting the drug *inside* a lipid, use the **Plant Extract itself** to synthesize the nanoparticle (e.g., Green Silver/Gold NPs, or Chitosan-coated Phyto-nanoparticles).
*   **Methodology (Wet Lab):**
    *   Mix Plant Extract + Metal Salt -> Nanoparticles (Plant acts as reducing agent).
    *   Test these NPs for Anti-Alzheimer's activity.
*   **Fit:** Justifies **"Phytochemical & ... Lead Formulation Optimization"** in a single novel step.
