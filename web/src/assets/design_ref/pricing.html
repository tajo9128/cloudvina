Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BioDockify pricing and features.

What happens to my unused credits?

For paid plans, credits never expire! You can use them at your own pace. Free plan credits expire after 30 days, and monthly free credits refresh every 30 days.

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Is there a limit on job complexity?

No, all plans support the same molecular docking algorithms and complexity levels. The only difference is the number of jobs you can run and processing priority.

Do you offer institutional discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for educational institutions and research organizations. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.

What file formats are supported?

We support all major molecular file formats including PDB, SDF, MOL2, and PDBQT. Our free converter tool helps you prepare files in the correct format.

Is my research data secure?

Absolutely! We use end-to-end encryption, secure AWS infrastructure, and automatically destroy all data after processing. Your research remains confidential.
